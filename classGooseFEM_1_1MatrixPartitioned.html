Detailed Description

Sparse matrix partitioned in an unknown and a prescribed part.

In particular: \( \begin{bmatrix} A_{uu} & A_{up} \\ A_{pu} & A_{pp} \end{bmatrix} \)

See VectorPartitioned() for bookkeeping definitions.
